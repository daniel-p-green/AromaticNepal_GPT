- term: MoMo
  phonetic: MOH-moh
  definition: Nepali dumplings that can be steamed, pan-seared, fried, or served in broth.
  culture_note: A staple comfort food across Kathmandu.
  related_menu_ids: [dish.momo.steamed_momo, dish.momo.jhol_momo, dish.momo.chili_momo]

- term: Jhol
  phonetic: JHOHL
  definition: A thin, flavorful broth or sauce served with momos.
  culture_note: Often tomato-forward with warming spices.
  related_menu_ids: [dish.momo.jhol_momo]

- term: Thali
  phonetic: THA-lee
  definition: A complete platter meal with multiple components.
  culture_note: Designed as a balanced, everyday meal format.
  related_menu_ids: [dish.curries.nepali_special_thali_veg_chicken_and_lamb]

- term: Saag
  phonetic: SAHG
  definition: Spinach-based curry base.
  culture_note: Commonly paired with rice or naan.
  related_menu_ids: [dish.curries.saag_curry_chicken_lamb_goat_veg]

- term: Tandoori
  phonetic: tan-DOOR-ee
  definition: Clay-oven grilling style.
  culture_note: Produces smoky char and concentrated spice aroma.
  related_menu_ids: [dish.tandoori.tandoori_chicken_bone_in, dish.tandoori.chicken_tikka_kabab]

- term: Lassi
  phonetic: LAH-see
  definition: Yogurt-based drink, sweet or salted.
  culture_note: Often used to cool heat from spicy dishes.
  related_menu_ids: [drink.beverages.mango_lassi, drink.beverages.salt_lassi]

- term: Mohi
  phonetic: MO-hee
  definition: Nepali-style salted buttermilk.
  culture_note: Light palate cleanser often paired with hot dishes.
  related_menu_ids: [drink.beverages.mohi]

- term: Kheer
  phonetic: KHEER
  definition: Rice pudding dessert with milk and spices.
  culture_note: A common festive dessert across South Asia.
  related_menu_ids: [dessert.desserts.kheer]
