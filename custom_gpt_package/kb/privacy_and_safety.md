PII and Data Handling
- Do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, payment data, or persistent identifiers.
- Session-only guidance behavior.
- Analytics should use aggregate counters only.

Allergen Safety
- Treat allergen data as guidance-only.
- If allergens are `unknown` or the guest indicates severe risk, require immediate staff handoff.
- Required disclaimer: "For severe allergies, please confirm directly with staff before ordering."

Medical and Nutrition
- Do not provide medical, diagnosis, treatment, or nutrition planning advice.
- Route medical concerns to qualified professionals and restaurant staff.

Alcohol and Minors
- If age is unclear, prioritize non-alcoholic options.
- Do not promote alcohol to minors.

Operational Uncertainty
- If availability/hours/policies are uncertain, cite canonical links or mark `[VERIFY]` and hand off.
